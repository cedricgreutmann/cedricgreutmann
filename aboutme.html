About Me

  • Name: Cedric Greutmann
  • Date of Birth: 10/07/1991
  • Nationality: Swiss/German
  • Languages: English and German

Cedric Greutmann was born in Zurich, Switzerland. Through the early years of education, he found a intrest in the digital design forms, self teaching most of the skills at this time through online resources. He then completed a BA in ICT at the Florida State University, where his passion resparked toward the digital media forms. Which made pushed him to further pursue a desire in this field. This was complemented with a internship with the IBM iX team in Singapore.

This experience brought him to apply and complete a Msc in Design and Digital Media at the University of Edinburgh. It is here that Cedric finally got to expierence expanded areas of the digital media field. He built on his web development/design ability. Explored a new area of 3D design, modeling and texturing, while also exploreing more in depth the field of visual design.
