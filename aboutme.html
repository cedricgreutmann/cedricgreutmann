About Me

  • Name: Cedric Greutmann
  • Date of Birth: 10.07.1991
  • Nationality: Swiss/German
  • Languages: English and German

Cedric Greutmann was born in Zurich, Switzerland. Through the early years of schooling Cedric, found a intrest in the digital design forms, self teaching most of the skills at this time through online resources. He then completed a BA in ICT at the Florida State University, where his passion resparked toward the digital media forms. Which made him then further pursue a desire in this field. This was further complemented with a internship with the IBM iX team in Singapore.

Which brought him to complete a Msc in Design and Digital Media at the University of Edinburgh. It iis here that Cedric finally got to expierence more areas of the digital media field that he found so interesting so many years ago. He built on his web development/design ability. Explored a new are of 3D design, moddeling and texturing, while also exploreing more in depth the field of visual design.
